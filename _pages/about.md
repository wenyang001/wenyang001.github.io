I am a **Research Fellow** at the **Hyundai–NTU–A\*STAR Corporate Lab**, Nanyang Technological University, supervised by Assoc. Prof. Wai Kin (Adams) Kong. I build **vision-based robotic perception systems** for smart manufacturing, focusing on open-world detection and segmentation, 6D pose estimation, and grasp pose generation for reliable industrial part picking at HMGICS.

My broader expertise is in **reliable visual AI under real-world constraints**, including degraded media, lightweight deployment, visual anomalies, and previously unseen objects. During my Ph.D. at NTU, advised by Prof. Chau Lap-Pui (IEEE Fellow) and Assoc. Prof. Yap Kim-Hui, I developed deep learning methods for corrupted-media recovery, image super-resolution, and visual anomaly detection.

I have authored **20 publications**, including first-author work at CVPR and two IEEE TMM papers, with **300+ citations** and an h-index of 8. My DATE paper *HolyLight* has received 140+ citations. I also review for CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, NeurIPS, and ACM MM.

**I am currently exploring industry opportunities** as a Research Scientist, Applied Scientist, or Computer Vision / Robotics Engineer, especially roles that turn research prototypes into reliable real-world intelligent systems.
