**Hyundai–NTU–A\*STAR Corporate Lab** · *Nov 2025 – Present*

Building the perception stack for reliable robotic part picking at HMGICS: open-world object detection and segmentation, 6D pose estimation, and grasp pose generation for cluttered industrial scenes.

---

**Schaeffler–NTU Corporate Lab, Phase III: Multimodal Human-Robot Interaction** · *Jun 2025 – Nov 2025*

Benchmarked multimodal 3D human pose estimation and developed motion-retargeting methods for human–robot collaborative tasks in industrial environments.

---

**DSO National Laboratories — Bitstream-Based Digital Watermarking** · *Dec 2024 – Jun 2025*

Built a robust encoder–decoder framework for digital watermarking with tamper resistance under JPEG compression and noise. Proposed SSH-Net, a self-supervised hybrid network for noisy watermark removal without paired training data. [[JVCIR 2025]](/publications/)

---

**Schaeffler–NTU Corporate Lab, Phase II: Visual Sensing, Detection & Analytics** · *Mar 2024 – Dec 2024*

Developed deep learning solutions for object detection and human–object interaction (HOI) recognition in industrial environments, including open-vocabulary methods and cross-level HOI distillation from multimodal LLMs. [[KBS 2025, ACM MM 2025, ICIP 2024, EMNLP 2024]](/publications/)

---

**NRF Singapore — Computer-Aided Image & Video Data Analysis in Flash Memory** · *Jan 2021 – Mar 2024*

Designed learning-based frameworks to restore corrupted images and videos from damaged flash memory. Work covers JPEG bitstream restoration, bitstream-corrupted video recovery, and multimedia file forensics. [[CVPR 2023, NeurIPS 2023, IEEE TMM 2024, DCC 2023]](/publications/)
