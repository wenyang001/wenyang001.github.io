Wenyang Liu

Research Fellow · Nanyang Technological University, Singapore · Hyundai–NTU–A*STAR Corporate Lab

📧 liuwenyang.sun@gmail.com

🇸🇬 Singapore

I am a Research Fellow at the Hyundai–NTU–A*STAR Corporate Lab, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. My work focuses on robust visual perception for smart manufacturing — including industrial object segmentation, open-world detection, and foundation model adaptation for real production environments.

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from NTU in 2025, supervised by Prof. Chau Lap-Pui (IEEE Fellow) and Assoc. Prof. Yap Kim-Hui. Prior to NTU, I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. from Chongqing University, China.

Research interests: Image/Video Restoration · Super-Resolution · Degradation Modeling · Visual Anomaly Detection · Vision-Language Models · Industrial AI · Robotic Perception

Highlights: 18 publications · 300+ citations · h-index 8 · 1 first-author CVPR · 2 first-author IEEE TMM · NeurIPS · ACM MM

I am interested in Applied Scientist, Research Scientist, and Computer Vision Engineer roles at companies working on real-world vision systems, industrial AI, or multimodal foundation models.

    Wenyang Liu, Yi Wang, Kim-Hui Yap, and 1 more author
